AI Resources

I often get asked how to stay up to date with AI. Here are some of my sources.

Newsletters

I find email newsletters still to be a fantastic source of information. Easily skimmable. I usually learn 1-2 new things from each of these.

  • AI News: http://news.smol.ai/
  • Latent Space: https://www.latent.space/
  • Every: https://every.to/newsletter
  • Pragmatic Engineer: https://www.pragmaticengineer.com/
  • PulseMCP: https://www.pulsemcp.com/
  • Data Science Programming News - Run by Eric J Ma

Blogs/Websites (I use Feedly)
